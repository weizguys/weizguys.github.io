Special Valentines Brew Category for 2021

Sweetheart's Revenge will once again have a special Valentine’s Day Themed Brew category. Any type of Valentine’s Day themed beer, mead, or cider will be accepted. Bitter, Sour, Sweet, Catty … Create a beverage with the flavors that represent your feelings about Valentine’s Day or your Valentine. Entry is “Valentines Brew” on Reggie in the category pull down. Enter discount code “VALENTINES” to get an $8 discount on your Valentines Brew entry. Only Valentine’s Brews are eligible for the discount. Only 1 discount per entrant. No limit on entries per entrant except for the 350 entry limit of the competition. There will be a Special Award presented to the winner of this category in addition to a competition medal. Medals will be awarded for 1st, 2nd, & 3rd places subject to the competition Official Rules and Regulations & Judging, Stewarding, & Awards guidelines. These brews will be judged in a “Best of Show” format.
